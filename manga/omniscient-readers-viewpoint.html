SUMMARY

Kim Dokja is an ordinary office worker whose sole interest is reading his favorite web novel, 'Three Ways to Survive the Apocalypse.' The story follows the journey of the novel's protagonist, Yoo Jonghyuk, a hero who keeps looping through time to save the world.

When the real world suddenly transforms into the setting of the novel, Kim Dokja gains an extraordinary advantage: he knows the entire plot of the world that has now become reality. Armed with this knowledge, he navigates the now-dangerous world, using his unique position as the 'omniscient reader' to change the course of the story and help save the lives of protagonist Yoo Jonghyuk and other major characters from the novel.
