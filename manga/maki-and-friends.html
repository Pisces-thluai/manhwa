SUMMARY

Follow the daily adventures of Maki, a cheerful high school student, and her diverse group of friends as they navigate the ups and downs of teenage life. Each with their own distinct personality, Maki's friends bring color and excitement to the otherwise ordinary school days.

What starts as simple high school comedy gradually evolves into a heartwarming story about friendship, growth, and the unbreakable bonds formed during these formative years. As the story progresses, the characters face various challenges that test their relationships and help them discover who they truly want to be.
